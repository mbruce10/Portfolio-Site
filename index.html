About

I'm Meaghan.
A Creator.
A Problem Solver.
A Front End Developer.

My interest in building digital products began with my career in data analytics. After several years of creating analytics dashboards and managing databases, I wanted to expand my work into creating a variety of user experiences and digital products, both for business and personal use. Once I started my journey into front end development, I was hooked!

What excites me the most about design and development is being able to create things that have purpose and solve real problems. Beyond apps and websites, I have a passion for creating experiences and solutions that help people, whether it’s helping them market their business, buy something online, or wind down at the end of the day with a coloring app. I can’t wait to discover more possibilities with the emergence of AI, and am fascinated with this new landscape.

Everything I have done has been a vital stepping stone for where I am today, and I find fulfillment and inspiration in the infinite opportunities for creativity and innovation.

When I’m not immersed in the world of technology, I love to bake, play piano, and travel anywhere with clear blue water.

One of my favorite quotes from Pablo Picasso:

“Learn the rules like a pro so you can break them like an artist.”
